# Stonevale Brand Kit - Quote App
**For Noah | quote.stonevaleexterior.com | 2026-06-11**
**Goal: the quote app should feel like a page OF stonevaleexterior.com, not a separate tool. Match the main site's look: dark charcoal, cream, gold accents, serif headings.**

---

## Colors (exact, non-negotiable)

| Use | Hex |
|---|---|
| Dark surfaces, headers, footers, price blocks | `#1c1c1c` |
| Page background, light surfaces | `#f0ebe3` (cream) |
| Accent: prices, rules, hover states, small caps labels | `#c4a265` (gold) |
| Body text on cream | `#1c1c1c` primary, `#4a443c` secondary |
| Muted text on dark | `#a99c84` |
| Variable/personalized values (address, name) when highlighted | `#8a6a1f` italic |

White `#ffffff` only for QR backgrounds and form fields. No other colors. No gradients except a subtle dark overlay on photos.

## Type

Google Fonts, both free:
- **Cinzel** (weights 600, 700) - headings, tier names, prices. Never body copy.
- **Raleway** (400, 500, 600, 700 + italics) - everything else.

Embed: `https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Cinzel:wght@600;700&family=Raleway:ital,wght@0,400;0,500;0,600;0,700;1,400;1,500&display=swap`

Scale guide: H1 ~28-34px Cinzel 600; section labels 11-12px Raleway 700, uppercase, letterspaced ~1.5px, gold; body 16-17px Raleway 400, line-height 1.55; prices Cinzel 600 with a small uppercase "FROM" label above in Raleway.

## The three packages (exact names, always with "The")

1. **The Exterior Refresh** - "The first visit. The whole exterior brought back."
2. **The Full Home Detail** - "The entire property, done right in one visit."
3. **The Estate Care Plan** - "Year-round care, so you never think about it." (displayed monthly, soft-sold - shown, never pushed)

Price display: "from $X,XXX" - low anchor, gold, Cinzel. Always near it: "Final figure confirmed on a short walkthrough."

## Voice (matters as much as the colors)

Steward + craftsman + educator. Founder-voiced - Garrett is a real person on the page, not a brand mascot.

DO say: care, steward, fine homes, well-built, the right method for every surface, honest numbers, measured, confirmed.
NEVER: "luxury" as an adjective for the service, "limited spots" or any fake scarcity, hype/clickbait, the dead slogan "soft wash not pressure wash" (we DO pressure wash concrete - the line is "the right method for every surface"), em dashes (use " - "), invented claims or reviews.
